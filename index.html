About

What do you get by putting together five people with a passion for rock music?
Just Ordinary Group Stuff.

Formed in 2020, the band proves they're not ordinary at all: each member's influences blend into songs that carry shades of many genres and reflect the contemporary energy of their hometown, Milan.
